LMWFY Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2025

Your privacy matters. LMWFY ("Let Me Write For You") is designed to help you write. We collect minimal data and never sell your information.

What Data We Collect

LMWFY collects the following data:

  • Writing Content: Text you write in the app is sent to our AI servers for processing when you use the AI writing features. This data is processed to generate writing suggestions and is not stored permanently.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to LMWFY Premium, purchase data is processed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat (our subscription management service) to manage your subscription status.

What Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal identifiers
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use advertising or analytics tracking
  • We do not share or sell your data to third parties

How Your Writing Data is Used

When you use AI writing features:

  • Your text is sent securely to our AI processing servers
  • The AI generates writing suggestions based on your input
  • Your text is not stored permanently on our servers
  • We do not use your writing to train AI models

Local Storage

Your stories and writing content are stored locally on your device. This data remains on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

LMWFY uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat processes subscription data in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • Apple App Store: For in-app purchases, governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transmission to our servers, including HTTPS encryption.

Children's Privacy

LMWFY is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
